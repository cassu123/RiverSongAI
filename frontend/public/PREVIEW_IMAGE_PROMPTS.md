# Preview Backdrop Image Prompts

Generate each image at **21:9 / 3440×1440** (or larger, same aspect). Save into
`frontend/public/` with the exact filename below. The engine picks them up
automatically — no code change needed.

Shared rules for every prompt:
- **NO PEOPLE. NO TEXT. NO WATERMARKS. NO LOGOS.**
- Style: cinematic concept art, photorealistic matte painting, volumetric lighting,
  atmospheric perspective, 8K detail.
- Composition: focal mass roughly **left of center**, right third left open for UI.
- Safe zone: keep critical detail within central 70% vertical, central 80% horizontal.

## 1. `caladan_base.jpg` — ATREIDES / CALADAN

A storm-lashed ocean cliff coastline on the Atreides homeworld Caladan from Dune.
Towering wet black-basalt cliffs rise from a violent dark grey-green ocean, waves
crashing against their base sending up massive plumes of white sea spray. Castle
Caladan sits silhouetted on the highest cliff — a brutal stone fortress with a few
warm amber windows lit against the storm.

The sky is heavy storm clouds — slate grey shifting to teal where lightning
illuminates them from within. Rain sheets diagonally across the entire frame.
Distant lightning flickers behind the clouds. Mist and sea spray soften the
horizon. The mood is noble and melancholic, the ancestral home being left behind.

Aspect ratio: 21:9 ultrawide. Focal: castle silhouette left-of-center on its cliff,
right side open ocean fading into mist.

Inspired by Denis Villeneuve's Dune films, the Atreides homeworld scenes, the
North Atlantic coast in winter, painterly seascapes.
