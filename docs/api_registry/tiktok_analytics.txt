SERVICE: TikTok for Developers (Analytics API)
WHAT IT DOES: Lets River Song pull your TikTok account followers, video views, likes, and estimated revenue from the TikTok Creator/Business analytics dashboard.
HOW TO GET IT:
  1. Go to developers.tiktok.com and sign in with your TikTok account.
  2. Click "Manage Apps" -> "Connect an app".
  3. Fill in the app name (e.g. "River Song"), description, and category (choose "Analytics").
  4. Under "Products", add "Login Kit" and "Research API" or "Content Posting API" depending
     on your account type. For analytics you need at minimum "Login Kit".
  5. In the app settings, copy the Client Key and Client Secret.
     These go in TIKTOK_CLIENT_KEY and TIKTOK_CLIENT_SECRET.
  6. Set the redirect URI to: http://localhost:8000/callback/tiktok
     (or whatever your River Song server address is -- must match exactly)
  7. Submit the app for review. Basic analytics scopes are approved within 1-3 business days.
  8. Once approved, visit the OAuth flow URL your River Song server generates to authorize your
     TikTok account. River Song will store the resulting access token automatically.
     The token goes in TIKTOK_ACCESS_TOKEN.
  Link: https://developers.tiktok.com
VARIABLE NAME: TIKTOK_CLIENT_KEY, TIKTOK_CLIENT_SECRET, TIKTOK_ACCESS_TOKEN
WHERE TO PUT IT: .env
NOTES:
  - TikTok requires a real app review even for personal use. Plan for 1-5 business days.
  - Access tokens expire after 24 hours. River Song will need to implement refresh token
    rotation using your Client Key + Client Secret once the login flow is built.
  - The "Research API" is for academic/business use and has separate approval requirements.
    For personal dashboard analytics, Login Kit + user.info.basic scope is sufficient.
  - If your account is a Business Account (not Creator), use the TikTok Business API instead:
    business-api.tiktok.com -- the credentials are the same but the endpoints differ.
  - Creator accounts can access video metrics (views, likes, comments, shares).
    Revenue data (gifts, LIVE earnings) requires additional "Creator Marketplace" scopes.
