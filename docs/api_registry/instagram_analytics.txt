SERVICE: Instagram Graph API (Meta Developer Platform)
WHAT IT DOES: Lets River Song pull your Instagram Business or Creator account followers, impressions, reach, and post engagement metrics.
HOW TO GET IT:
  1. Your Instagram account MUST be a Business or Creator account, not a personal account.
     To convert: Instagram app -> Settings -> Account -> Switch to Professional Account.
  2. Your Instagram account MUST be linked to a Facebook Page you manage.
     Instagram app -> Settings -> Account -> Linked Accounts -> Facebook.
     Or from Facebook: Settings -> Instagram -> Connect Account.
  3. Go to developers.facebook.com and sign in with your Facebook account.
  4. Click "My Apps" -> "Create App". Choose "Business" as the use case.
  5. Fill in the app name (e.g. "River Song Analytics") and your email.
  6. In the app dashboard, click "Add Product" and add "Instagram Graph API".
  7. In the app settings under "Basic", copy the App ID and App Secret.
     These go in INSTAGRAM_APP_ID and INSTAGRAM_APP_SECRET.
  8. Set the OAuth redirect URI to: http://localhost:8000/callback/instagram
  9. In Graph API Explorer (developers.facebook.com/tools/explorer), generate a User Access
     Token with these permissions: instagram_basic, instagram_manage_insights, pages_read_engagement.
  10. Exchange the short-lived token for a long-lived token using:
      GET https://graph.facebook.com/oauth/access_token?grant_type=fb_exchange_token
          &client_id={INSTAGRAM_APP_ID}&client_secret={INSTAGRAM_APP_SECRET}
          &fb_exchange_token={short_lived_token}
      Copy the result. This goes in INSTAGRAM_ACCESS_TOKEN.
  11. Get your Instagram User ID:
      GET https://graph.facebook.com/me/accounts?access_token={INSTAGRAM_ACCESS_TOKEN}
      Find the Page connected to your Instagram. Then:
      GET https://graph.facebook.com/{page_id}?fields=instagram_business_account&access_token={token}
      The id returned goes in INSTAGRAM_USER_ID.
  Link: https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram-api
VARIABLE NAME: INSTAGRAM_APP_ID, INSTAGRAM_APP_SECRET, INSTAGRAM_ACCESS_TOKEN, INSTAGRAM_USER_ID
WHERE TO PUT IT: .env
NOTES:
  - Long-lived tokens are valid for 60 days. River Song will need a token refresh flow once
    the login integration is built. Meta provides a refresh endpoint before expiry.
  - The app will be in "Development mode" initially, which limits it to users listed as
    Developers or Testers in the app. For personal use this is fine -- just add yourself.
  - Insights (impressions, reach) are only available for the past 30 days maximum.
  - "Revenue" is not directly available from the Instagram API. River Song will show
    engagement metrics only unless linked to a Meta Business Suite monetization account.
  - App review (for going to Live mode) is not required if you are only analyzing your own account.
